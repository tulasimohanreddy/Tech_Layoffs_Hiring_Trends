# 🚀 Excited to Share My Tech Layoffs & Hiring Trends Analytics Dashboard! 📊💼

I recently developed an **Interactive Analytics Dashboard** using a **Tech Layoffs & Hiring Trends Dataset (12,000 records | 2024–2026)** to transform workforce data into actionable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

## 🔍 Dashboard Features

✅ Interactive KPI Metrics
✅ Industry-wise Layoff Performance Analysis
✅ Hiring Trend & Market Condition Analysis
✅ AI Automation Impact & Replacement Risk Insights
✅ Layoff Reason Breakdown
✅ Job Security & Employee Sentiment Analysis
✅ Top In-Demand Role Tracking
✅ Dynamic Filters for Data Exploration
✅ Interactive Visualizations & Business Recommendations

## 📊 Key Insights

📌 **41.7%** of companies are currently under a **Hiring Freeze**, while only **4.1%** are hiring aggressively.

📌 **AI Automation** has emerged as the **second-largest cause of layoffs**, nearly matching Overhiring Corrections, indicating a structural shift rather than a temporary market cycle.

📌 The average **AI Replacement Risk Score** stands at **7.2/10** across industries.

📌 During recessionary periods, **41%** of companies downsize, while only **1%** increase hiring, making market conditions the strongest predictor of hiring behavior.

📌 Despite widespread hiring freezes, **Machine Learning Engineers** and **Data Scientists** remain among the most sought-after professionals.

📌 **50.2%** of open positions are remote, highlighting that remote work has become a standard expectation rather than a workplace perk.

📌 Job Security Scores remain relatively consistent across company sizes (**5.76–5.85**), suggesting that startups are not significantly riskier than large technology companies in the current environment.
